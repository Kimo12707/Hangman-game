## **How to Play**
1. The game selects a secret word.
2. You have **10 guesses** to figure it out.
3. Enter a **single letter** per turn.
4. Incorrect guesses reduce the number of attempts:
   - **Consonants:** Lose 1 guess.
   - **Vowels:** Lose 2 guesses.
5. Use **'!'** to reveal a missing letter (costs 3 guesses).
6. Win by guessing all letters before running out of attempts!

## **Example Gameplay**
```
Welcome to Hangman!
I am thinking of a word that is 5 letters long.
-----------------------------------------
You have 10 guesses left
Available letters: abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz
Please guess a letter: e
Good guess! e****
-----------------------------------------
```
